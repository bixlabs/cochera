About Us

Our Story

Digital Garage was founded March 1, 2016 with a commitment to serve the DevOps community of small, innovative companies and teams that need a simple, easy, and flexible platform for developing, testing, and staging software applications.

We provide a cloud-based platform as a service (PaaS) that supports several programming languages and functions as well as integrated DevOps build, run, deploy, and manage applications.

At Digital Garage we believe the development community’s future is limitless and growing. Developers and software engineers are becoming the decision-makers. Our goal is to build a loyal customer base for the wave of startups as they develop and test new software products. Our customer outreach will be anchored by a community of users around the Digital Garage brand to drive word-of-mouth traffic.

Digital garage is especially passionate about working with disadvantaged communities to grow skills, jobs, and professional engagement to allow people of all shapes and sizes in whatever geographic market to tap into technology as a new career option.

"Many application developers are embracing a new wave in software architecture and open source tools, namely container-based microservices. Digital Garage is positioned right alongside this group to help fill those needs.” — John McCawley, Co-founder and CEO, the Digital Garage.

Partnerships

Digital Garage will pursue partners across tech markets in the U.S and abroad. Partnerships will be carefully selected to ensure they excite and energize the Digital Garage user community, Partners will provide add-ons to Digital Garage’s offerings and will also attract additional users while retaining the ones we have. Partners will tie-into Digital Garage’s overall mission of being an open, simple, and flexible software testing and building environment with a laser focus on the needs of the individual developer.

  • Training Partners such as on-line software development training partners to create awareness and provide credibility.
  • System Integrators comprised of mid- to large system integrators and development designers to create awareness, support, etc.
  • Service Add-On in areas of infrastructure or production.
